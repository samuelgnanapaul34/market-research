How to read the whole map: vertical depth or infrastructure plumbing wins; horizontal apps are gorilla territory. The genuinely open ground sits in (a) the trust / governance / identity / memory infra the failure stats expose, and (b) boring, legacy, regulated, or fragmented verticals the funded clones avoid.
