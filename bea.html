FAQ

The questions buying & IT teams ask.

Do we have to replace our ERP or migrate data?
No. Bea connects read-only to your existing SAP environment and acts through sanctioned write paths only when you turn that on. No migration, no rip-and-replace.
Is it autonomous, or do we stay in control?
You choose. Every agent runs in "propose" mode first — it drafts the action for a human to approve. As trust builds, you let it act touchless on the cases you're comfortable with. The judgement stays with your buyers.
How is this different from SAP's own AI?
ERP copilots are great at decisions (assortment, forecasting) and ship as a toolkit you build on. Bea is a finished agent that does the authoring busywork — exceptions, SKU setup, onboarding — and it's tuned to work in heavily customised estates where horizontal copilots struggle.
How fast is value, really?
The read-only Exception-Loss Audit returns a quantified £ figure within about two weeks. Agents typically go live shortly after — weeks, not quarters.
What does it cost?
Subscription, priced against the labour it gives back — typically a fraction of the cost it offsets, for a payback measured in months. Start with one agent; add more as you expand.
Is our data secure?
Read-only by default, least-privilege access, a full audit trail on every action, and approval gates wherever you want them. Your IT team controls what the agents can touch.
