What the agent does per article

Parses the supplier's new-line file (PDF, Excel, form or portal) → drafts the full article master: merchandise-category hierarchy placement, EAN validation against GS1, dimensions & weights from the spec sheet, VAT class, season/theme flags, supplier cost and a margin check against the category floor → flags anything missing and drafts the supplier chase → on your approval, creates the article via the sanctioned SAP path and confirms listing. It never decides the range — assortment stays your call.
